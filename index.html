Safety And Quality



Atrium Logistics has embraced greater security measures than the North American norm to further comfort our partners and customers. Our fleet consists of modern vehicles with the most recent safety and preventive technology. Additionally, in order to keep them among the safest drivers on the road, our drivers constantly participate in defensive driving instruction. Automatic and real-time contact with the supervisory team is made possible by the on-board equipment. This enables us to respond quickly in order to mitigate any danger to the safety of our fleet and the people who work on board. In order to comply with the criteria of the ISO 9001 standard, Atrium Logistics has also started the certification process for its quality management system. This dedication enables us to consistently evaluate our working practises in order to uphold very high standards.
